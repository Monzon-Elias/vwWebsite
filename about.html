About this website

The purpose of this web site is to share my satisfaction for having lived a very interesting, and totally self-taught experience with the Volkswagen air-cooled mechanics. This experience has served me as a mind sanitizer in a tough period of my life.

About my experience

My learning experience was from mainly from videos that taught the know-how of many repairs to a VW air-cooled. My practical experience was in my own car when I applied all I was learning. All this, born from the need to make my own repairs, for lack of money and time to take my car to the mechanic.

My sources

Tonella and Higgins are my two main virtual mentors who taught me everything I needed to start gaining my own experience and knowledge about the wonderful VW air-cooled. Thanks to them, I have learned to do most of the repairs in all the systems of my beetles. I think about 80% of everything I know now.

Historic glimpse

I also share a brief glimpse of the main car models that Volkswagen has produced under the air-cooled philosophy. These are divided into high-end and low-end models.
