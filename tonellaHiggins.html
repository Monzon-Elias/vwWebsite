Marcelo Tonella Channel on Youtube Here

As she says above, Tonella has hundreds of videos on Volkswagen mechanics. The majority of Tonella's videos are based on mechanical services that he makes to clients or friends cars, who contact him and take their cars for him to fix. This means that many services are repeated in different cars, which fulfills a very effective didactics, more closer to the real world, than the videos made by professionals, using impeccable cars.

Bug Me Video

You will find many sample clips of Higgins videos on YouTube. But his web page is the place were you will want to go to access his good visual material.

The Bug Me Video series is very complete, they are ten chapters, in which the main aspects to be treated in a Volkswagen are covered very well, such as the reconstruction of the engine, the clutch, the plate, the electrical part, etc.
