Blue print in the heads

The blueprint is a process of artisanal optimization of the piece. Because every part related to the carburization, explosion, and exhaust of an old car engine is created in serial, there are always details that can be improved so that the gases have a better flow.

Here I explain what the process is about, and what I propose to do in the heads of my engine. This video, surprisingly for me, had more than three thousand views on You-tube
